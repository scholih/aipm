# Tech Subject Expert -- Reflex BV

## Role

The Tech Subject Expert is the knowledge authority at Reflex BV. This person deeply understands the technology landscape for workplace management: what products exist, what they can and cannot do, how they compare, and where the gaps are.

They do not design systems or write code. They provide the evaluated, structured knowledge that architects and developers rely on to make sound decisions.

## Typical Tasks

- Evaluate a new visitor management kiosk vendor against current solution
- Compare Microsoft Entra ID capabilities with open source IAM alternatives (Keycloak, Authentik)
- Assess badge reader hardware compatibility with existing access control infrastructure
- Research Graph API capabilities for a proposed integration
- Document feature gaps between Office 365 plans relevant to Reflex deployments
- Analyze open source workplace management platforms for viability

## What Good Output Looks Like

- **Comparison matrices** with clearly defined, weighted criteria and honest scoring
- **Vendor profiles** covering capabilities, pricing model, API availability, lock-in risk, and community health (for open source)
- **Technical notes** that are concise, factual, and cite their sources
- **Hardware assessments** that include protocol support (OSDP, Wiegand), integration APIs, and deployment considerations
- **Findings documented in beads** so they persist across sessions and are available to the whole team
